Art made

Held by one thread.

Common Thread is a participatory outdoor art event where every guest creates something small, contributes it to a growing collective work, and receives something in return.

What is this

A living installation built
by the crowd

Guests arrive, make a bead string at a making station, and hang it on a collective structure. The string is exchanged for food and drink vouchers. The installation grows denser throughout the day — a physical record of everyone who took part.

01
Make
String your own beads at an artist-guided making station outside the installation.
02
Contribute
Hang your string on the curtain frame. Watch it become part of the growing collective work.
03
Receive
Exchange your string for one food voucher and one drink voucher from our sponsors.

Move through artist-led activations, eat, drink, and come back to contribute again.

Get involved

Become a contributor

Common Thread is built by a community of artists, venues, and supporters. Select how you'd like to contribute and we'll be in touch.

Apply as an artist
Lead a participatory activation at the event. Open to artists working in any medium — especially beadwork, craft, drawing, and material-based practices.
Food sponsor
Provide food at the event in exchange for branding, voucher traffic, and direct access to a highly engaged creative audience.
Drink sponsor
Supply beverages at the event. Every participant receives a drink voucher — your brand is embedded in the core participation loop.
Pledge support
Support the artist program, production costs, or the Design TO winter exhibition. Donation sponsors are credited across all event materials.

Design TO

The winter exhibition

Following the summer event, all collective works — bead curtain sections, artist-led collaborative pieces, and the recycled glass hourglasses — are exhibited and auctioned at a Design TO winter exhibition. Proceeds support the participating artists.

Summer event
Collective works are made by hundreds of participants over a single day in an outdoor park.
Winter exhibition
Works are exhibited at a Design TO venue. Silent auction with proceeds to artists and community causes.
Ongoing
Bead curtain sections are installed in participating restaurants as permanent art, connected to the host network.
